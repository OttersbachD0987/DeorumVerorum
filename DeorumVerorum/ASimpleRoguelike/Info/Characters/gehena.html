Gehena

Male Human, 67

Gehena served as a high ranking general in the Ekari Kingdom before the shattering. After the shattering Gehena rallied the Imperial guards and the capital into the nation of Kelaph, which he ruled through martial law. This lasted until 35 AS, when during the war of opposition, the capital was bombarded with curses, and Gehena was slain.
