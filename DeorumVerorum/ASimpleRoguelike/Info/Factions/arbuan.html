Arbuan

The Arbuan are the last remaining remnants of the Ekari kingdom before the great shattering and the arrival of Epitaph and Epithet. Due to their remaining resources being lesser than they once were due to the multiple wars between them and the other remnants, their focus shifted to maintaining the people they had, which led to the development of multiple war machines, and the eventual beginning of the Eternity Program, which led to the creation of Rotnots.
