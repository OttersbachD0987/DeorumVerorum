Ilec

Ilec were a short lived offspring of the shattered Ekari Kingdom, lasting for a few decades before getting into a war with Kelaph that ended in both of their destructions in 35 AS. Ilec was formed mainly by the holy church after the shattering, and was a Theocratic group led by the holy patron.
