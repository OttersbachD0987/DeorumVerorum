Nyalanth

Antediluvian Leviathan

Theme: _ by Peritune(PLACEHOLDER)

The Nyalanths are a species on the decline, their numbers dwindling since the Arciec era. Despite this those that remain are the most dangerous hunters of the waters they inhabit. Nylanths have three hind fins they use for propulsion, and two long tentacles that span the length of their body, and end in two leave shaped flippers. Nylanthanths have large eyes, and swim at incredible speeds, letting out deep bellowing roars. The Agarthin's worship them like deities.
