Rotnot

Undead Construct

Theme:

The Rotnot is a type of undead known as a Necrodemis. The Rotnot was the first of the Arbuan’s Eternity Programs results, and made up the bulk of the Arbuan’s early forces. The majority of Rotnots still in operation today are maintained by Arbuan, however a few notable ones have gone rogue, and as such are high priority targets for reclamation for the Arbuan. The Rotnot’s tether is the preserved heart of the original person, which is kept in an isolation chamber which is suspended in Concentrated Ethereal Fluid. The heart container is then secured within the central chamber, and connected to the body of the Rotnot. Rotnots generally are equipped with either a sword, gatling gun, cannon, or another type of weapon.
